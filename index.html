BOOK SHOP

the greatest ever book shop in the world

Bookshop is an online bookstore with a mission to financially support local, independent bookstores. We believe that bookstores are essential to a healthy culture. They’re where authors can connect with readers, where we discover new writers, where children get hooked on the thrill of reading that can last a lifetime. They’re also anchors for our downtowns and communities.



list of books

Harry Potter
Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows is a rousing culmination of seven books' worth of page-turning plot

It doesn't rely solely on its previous installments to wow you. There are new characters, new dangers, and new twists to keep you guessing. It's a satisfying conclusion to a much-loved series.

Heaven Official's Blessing
Heaven Official's Blessing (or Tian Guan Ci Fu) is a beautiful, heart-breaking chinese novel that's sure to make you cry

At its core, tgcf is a story about a fallen god and his last believer; it's a story about devotion, love, loss, longing, fate and destiny, and the meaning of “forever” and “to believe”.

The Grandmaster of Demonic Cultivation
The Grandmaster of Demonic Cultivation (or Mo Dao Zu shi) is a chinese xianxia novel that's as hilarious as it is tragic

A murder mistery with a reincarnated main charecter, and everyone is Jin Ling's uncle.
